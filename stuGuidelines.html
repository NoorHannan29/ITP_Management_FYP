STUDENT GUIDELINES

1. Duration and Prerequisites

Duration: 3 months

Prerequisites:

  • Minimum 60 credit hours
  • Include Core, Specialization Core/Elective, Elective subjects only
  • Include credit transfer subjects under the same categories
  • Include current trimester subjects
  • Exclude University subjects

2. Objectives

  1. Expose students to real working environments and organizational structures
  2. Provide hands-on experience related to their field of study
  3. Introduce new technologies and soft skill development
  4. Foster universityâ€“industry collaboration
  5. Enhance graduate employability with relevant knowledge and skills

3. Training Scope

  • Tasks should align with student's field of specialization
  • Opportunities to apply academic knowledge in professional settings
  • Example activities:
    • Feasibility studies, system analysis & design, development
    • Testing, implementation, evaluation, and installation
    • Data processing, organizational reengineering
    • Soft skills improvement: communication, documentation, entrepreneurship

5. Steps in Industrial Training Process

  1. Register ITP via CLiC : Self Service â†’ Industrial Training â†’ Apply for ITP
  2. Update personal info: Self Service â†’ Campus Personal Info + Upload Resume
  3. Collect reference letter from faculty after updates (usually 1 week)
  4. Apply to companies with required documents; use company master list
  5. Upload offer letter, letter of undertaking, insurance copy on clic.mmu
  6. Await committee approval (subject registration upon approval)
  7. Faculty supervisors will be assigned before training begins
  8. Submit training form, logs, and final report to supervisor (templates in bulletin)

6. Placement Outside Malaysia

  • Subject to strict faculty verification
  • Approval may be granted for:
    • Globally recognized companies
    • Strong supporting documents (e.g., embassy verification)
  • Maintain regular contact with MMU and company supervisors
  • Must submit reports and do final presentation

7. Professional Conduct

  • Only accept one offer
  • Disciplinary action if student accepts and fails to report
  • For changing placement:
    • Consult Industrial Training Committee
    • Submit offer withdrawal letter for approval
    • Only switch after committee approval
